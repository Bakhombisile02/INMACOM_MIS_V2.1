5:T663,# Station Management

This section walks you through managing water monitoring stations and suggestion-based revision workflows.

---

## 1. Stations Registry

To view the complete list of monitoring stations in the basin, navigate to the **Stations** tab.

![Stations Registry](/screenshots/stations.png)

This table displays the station metadata, including:
- **Station Code:** Unique basin identifier (e.g. `X2H016`).
- **Name & Basin:** Geographic name and associated river system.
- **Latitude & Longitude:** Coordinate positions.
- **Suggester / Status:** Shows who suggested the station and its review status.

---

## 2. Suggesting Revisions (Clerks)

Data Entry Clerks cannot modify station metadata (such as names or coordinates) directly. Instead, they submit a **Revision Request**:

1. Click on the station name to view its details.
2. Click **Suggest Revision**.
3. Edit the metadata fields in the form and click **Submit Suggestion**.
4. The station's status becomes `pending_revision`.

---

## 3. Reviewing & Approving Revisions (Managers)

When a station is in `pending_revision` status, it appears in the manager's review queue.

### Manager Actions:
- **Approve:** Clicking **Approve** copies the suggested changes into the active station record and updates the status to `approved`.
- **Reject:** Clicking **Reject** opens a modal where the manager must provide a rejection note explaining the decision. The suggestion is discarded, and the active station remains unchanged.
