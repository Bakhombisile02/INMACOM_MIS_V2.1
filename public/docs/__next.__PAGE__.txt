Welcome to the official documentation and user support portal for the **Incomati and Maputo Basins Management Information System (INMACOM MIS)**. 

The INMACOM MIS is a transboundary water resources management platform tracking stations, water measurements, compliance with the Incomati and Maputo Agreement (IIMA), disaster incidents, and shared document library workflows across Mozambique, South Africa, and Eswatini.

---
